About

BP-06: 12 Principles of Animation is an animated CGI short that focuses on showcasing the principles of animation vicariously through two robots, Riley and Steve. This video takes a spin on the originally proposed 12 principles by Disney animators Ollie Johnston and Frank Thomas in 1981.

The Goal

The goal of this project was to showcase the 12 principles as one succinct narrative rather than individual scenes and to venture into the realm of 3D animation for the first time.
